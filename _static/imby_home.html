IMBY Neighborhood Energy

What is IMBY Energy?

IMBY Energy is a comprehensive Neighborhood Energy project in SE Portland, Oregon. IMBY Energy will provide renewable, carbon-neutral, thermal energy to a single block (14 homes) on SE IMBY.
